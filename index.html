About AVS Events

Gorakhpur's Most Trusted Event Decorator

एवीएस इवेंट डेकोरेशन सर्विस — where every celebration becomes a masterpiece.

At AVS Event Decoration Service, we believe your special moments deserve nothing but the best. Our passionate team of decorators works tirelessly to bring your vision to life — whether it's a lavish wedding, a joyful birthday, or a corporate gathering.

With 100+ five-star reviews and a reputation built on trust, creativity, and hard work, we've become Gorakhpur's go-to name for event decoration. Our clients love us for our polite staff, beautiful work, and unbeatable value.

  • Customised decoration themes tailored to your vision and budget
  • Fresh floral arrangements sourced daily for vibrant, fragrant setups
  • Professional team with 9+ years of decoration experience
  • Available for all occasions — weddings, birthdays, corporate events & more
  • Full setup & cleanup service — stress-free experience for you
